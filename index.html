Engineering Perspective on Life Below Water

The Mission: Life Below Water

Sustainable Development Goal 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development. Oceans provide key natural resources including food, medicines, and biofuels.

However, marine pollution—mostly from land-based activities—is reaching alarming levels, with an average of 13,000 pieces of plastic litter found on every square kilometer of ocean.

Engineering the Solution

As Mechanical Engineering students, we apply our technical knowledge to design systems that can protect our aquatic ecosystems:

⚙️ Waste Interceptors

Designing automated conveyor systems and floating barriers to collect debris before it enters the deep sea.

🔋 Green Propulsion

Developing energy-efficient marine engines and electric propulsion to reduce carbon emissions and oil spills.

🤖 Ocean Robotics

Utilizing fluid mechanics to design drones and AUVs (Autonomous Underwater Vehicles) for cleaning microplastics.
