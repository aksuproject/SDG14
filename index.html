What is SDG 14?

Sustainable Development Goal 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources. Oceans cover 70% of our planet and are vital for human life.

Reduce Pollution
Preventing plastic waste and chemicals from entering the sea.
Protect Ecosystems
Restoring coral reefs and protecting marine biodiversity.
Sustainable Fishing
Ending overfishing to maintain healthy fish populations.
